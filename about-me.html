About Me

Hello. My name is Louis Crawford(the French (and proper) spelling of the name), and I am a casual writer. I'm currently taking a full-time Bachelors of Creative Media Design at Massey University in Wellington. I like to write stories about the characters I never really got while growing up as a kid (disabled & queer characters). When I'm not stressing about my work load, I like to consume a concerning amount of media, play Dungeons and Dragons, ponder world domination, and think about the stories I want to write (emphasise on the word THINK).
